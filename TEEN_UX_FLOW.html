Mission Flow — Choose Your Own Adventure

The core experience. A relatable scenario unfolds. The teen reads, grounds themselves, and makes a choice. The app reveals what happens. Then they reflect — and share anonymously with their pack. 3-5 minutes, deeply personal.

Mission: Consequence + Reflect
Both paths give XP — Path A gives more, but Path B still rewards engagement. The point is that teens learn from BOTH choices. The "thinking trap spotted" card teaches the CBT concept without being clinical. The reflection is optional but incentivized (bonus XP for sharing). Anonymous by design.

Safety Layer — Always Running

Safety is never visible until needed. The system silently monitors daily signals, reflections, and toolbox usage for risk indicators. When triggered, it responds proportionally — from a gentle prompt to immediate crisis intervention.

💛

We want to make sure you're okay

We noticed you've been feeling low for a few days. That takes courage to show up every day.

Would any of these help?

💬 Talk to my mentor
📞 Crisis text line (741741)
🧰 Open my toolbox
